NEW AGE COURSE

New Age CTS Courses are designed to cater the demand of skilled manpower in newly emerging areas. These courses will be on the lines of CTS (Craftsman Training Scheme) pattern.

Technician (Electrical) NSQF LEVEL 4 (1 Year)

CITS COURSES

The objective of the Craft Instructor Training (CITS) is to train Instructors in the techniques of transferring hands-on skills, in order to train semi-skilled / skilled manpower for industry.
