New Age Course

CTS-New Age Course

The Institute conducts Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS-New Age Course) under the aegis of the National Council of Vocational Training (NCVT). Training is imparted under the yearly pattern. Admission to CTS Course is made in the month of July every year as per the admission policy prescribed by the Directorate General of Training (DGT). DGT announces the admission guidelines in newspapers, websites, and Admission forms are filled online. At NSTI Jodhpur Training under CTS in the following trades is imparted.
